Hylian Recipies

This page will show you all recipes that are normally made by Hylians.

Hint! Look in the Great Plateau, Central Hyrule and Necluda regions for the ingredients.

Recipe Name:         Items Required:       
Skewer
  • Any meat for a meat skewer
  • Any mushroom for a mushroom skewer
  • Any fish for a fish skewer
  • Any other seafood for a seafood skewer
Fruitcake
  • Any fruit
  • Tabantha wheat
  • Sugar
 ←  Fun fact! Fruitcake is said to be Princess Zelda's favourite food.
Rice balls
  • Rice
  • Any meat for meat rice balls
  • Any mushroom for mushroom rice balls
  • Any fish for seafood rice balls
  • Any vegetable or flower for vegetable rice balls
  • Monster extract (obtained from Kilton) for monster rice balls
   ←   Find the mushroom variation on a poster at East Wind General Store in Hateno Village.
Crepe
  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Add butter for a plain crepe
  • Add a wildberry for a wildberry crepe
  • Add courser bee honey for an energizing honey crepe

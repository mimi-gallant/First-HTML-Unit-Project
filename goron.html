Goron Recipes

Hint! Look in the Eldin region (Death Mountain and surrounding area) for most of the ingredients!

Recipe Name: Ingredients Required:
Curry
  • Goron Spice
  • Rice
  • Add any meat to it to make it "meat" curry
  • Add monster extract (obtained from Kilton) to make it "monster" curry
  • Add any seafood to it to make "seafood" curry.
  • Add an egg to it to make curry "pilaf"
Spiced meat skewer
  • Goron Spice
  • Any meat
Herb Saute
  • Goron Spice
  • Any vegetable
Rock-hard food
  • Wood
  • Any gemstone or rock
